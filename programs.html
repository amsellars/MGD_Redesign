About Our Programs

At Front Range Community College, students may take classes to earn either a Certificate or an Associate Degree. Credits from the State Guaranteed Education Courses are guaranteed to transfer to Colorado's public four-year institutions. Students may also take classes for personal enrichment or career enhancement.

 

Learn industry-standard software in our interactive multimedia lab.Build a foundation in illustration, photo manipulation, web graphics, animation, 3D modelling, and digital video production. Once you master the core classes, focus on a specific area of emphasis by taking classes designed to enhance your skills in your chosen discipline.

Degrees

Multimedia Technology degrees have four tracks. The faculty in the department can help to understand these better. Here are the four emphases:

  • Multimedia Animation
  • Multimedia Graphic Design
  • Multimedia Video
  • Multimedia Web

All degrees and certificates require:

  • Introduction to Multimedia

Most degrees and certificates require:

  • MGD 102 - Introduction to Multimedia
  • MGD 112 - Adobe Illustrator I
